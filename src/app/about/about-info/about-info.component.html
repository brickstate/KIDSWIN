System Description

This student management portal is meant to serve as a platform for tracking student milestones, form status, and attendance and behavior logs.


Two different views are provided for either a teacher or a parent. Teachers view more information of their students and internally are able to makr attendence and daily disciplines. Parent view captures data from Legal forms needed to be filled out for either Federal, County, State, or the KidsWin Facility
